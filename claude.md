## Workflow

1. Read the relevant parts of the codebase first.
2. Write a plan to `tasks/todo.md` as a markdown checklist.
   - Use `- [ ]` for pending items
   - Keep each item small and actionable
3. **STOP and wait for my approval before writing any code.**
4. After approval, complete todos one by one.
   - Mark each item `- [x]` when done
   - Give a brief explanation of what changed and why
5. Keep every change minimal. No large rewrites unless explicitly asked.
6. When all items are done, append a `## Review` section to `todo.md`
   summarizing what changed and any follow-up items.
